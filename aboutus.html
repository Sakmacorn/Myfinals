About Us

Kape Bartolina is proud to be one of the best local coffee stores established in the Philippines. Recognized as one of the top 100 coffee shops in the world, we are dedicated to delivering the finest coffee experience our customers can enjoy. Our journey began with a vision to showcase that the Philippines can stand shoulder-to-shoulder with global coffee destinations, especially those in the Americas, known for their rich coffee culture. We built Kape Bartolina not only to offer exceptional coffee but to celebrate Filipino heritage and craftsmanship in every cup. Over the years, we have grown from a small community cafe into a beloved institution that blends tradition with innovation, always striving to elevate the coffee scene in the country and inspire pride in local quality and flavor.
