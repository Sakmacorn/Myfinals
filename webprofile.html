Miguel M. Lulu

Aspiring Web Designer & Front-End Developer

Background & Experience

I'm a passionate and detail-oriented student with a focus on web design and front-end development. I started with the basics—HTML and CSS—and have since expanded my skills to include responsive design, user interface improvements, and the fundamentals of JavaScript. Along the way, through both academic projects and personal exploration, I've developed a strong eye for clean layouts and consistent, effective branding.

One project I'm especially proud of is this website—Kape Bartolina. I designed and built it from the ground up to showcase modern design principles, easy navigation, and a visually engaging experience.

Reflection

Building this website wasn’t easy—especially since I went with a coffee shop theme. There are just so many cool design ideas out there that I kept second-guessing which one to choose. Luckily, my experience with HTML and CSS helped me put it all together faster than I expected.

That said, every website I build seems to come with its own set of challenges. This time, getting the design to work on different screen sizes was tough. The mobile layout gave me the most trouble, but I figured it out in the end.

Creating Kape Bartolina really got me excited to keep learning and trying new things with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
